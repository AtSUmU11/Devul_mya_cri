— SECTION I —
KEYBOARD SECRETS

type these words on your keyboard while playing. you have 2.5 seconds between letters before the buffer resets. you don't need to press enter. it just listens.

JACKPOT any character
triggers a 30-second orb shower. fifty orbs every second, gold slash storm, floating JACKPOT text raining across the screen in different sizes. your combo keeps climbing the entire time. dante would approve.
reward — +50 orbs/sec for 30 seconds · +5 combo/sec · gold flash storm
PIZZA any character
pizza slices and text rain down from the top of the screen, drifting to the bottom. completely unhinged. very dante. includes the classic line.
reward — +100 orbs · +500 score · +20 combo · chaos
MOTIVATED any character
every single button label becomes "MOTIVATED!!!" for 10 seconds. maximum damage applied. floating motivation text floods the screen. expires with a quiet "...motivation expired." vergil would find this embarrassing.
reward — max damage for 10s · all buttons relabelled · floating text storm
SPARDA any character
screen floods deep purple, warp hits 35. ten dark slashes tear across. then the quotes: "Son of Sparda." "The Legendary Dark Knight." "...father." the game briefly becomes something heavier.
reward — +300 orbs · +2000 score · +40 combo · purple quote storm
VERGIL while playing DANTE
vergil appears as a ghost and steals exactly half your orbs. purple slashes tear through the screen. his quotes rain down — "foolishness." "pathetic." "this power is mine now." lore accurate.
reward — vergil takes 50% of your orbs. that's it. he just takes them.
VERGIL while playing VERGIL
"..." and +40 concentration. he approves of himself. nothing else happens. the restraint is the joke.
reward — +40 concentration · one quiet ellipsis
DANTE while playing VERGIL
red invasion. dante's quotes flood vergil's screen — "JACKPOT!" "...bro." "pizza time." "lol." concentration drops. combo bleeds. this is what vergil endures every time dante opens his mouth.
reward — -20% combo · -30 concentration · dante chaos on your screen
NERO while playing VERGIL
"...that's my boy." +40 concentration. vergil won't admit he's proud. but he gives you the concentration. that's basically the same thing.
reward — +40 concentration · one moment of cold paternal pride
KONAMI any character
the classic. everything maxed instantly. white flash storm, warp at 25, twelve screen-wide slashes. if JACKPOT is dante's cheat code, KONAMI is dante's cheat code but louder.
reward — +9999 score · +999 orbs · +60 combo · UNLIMITED POWER
— SECTION I.V —
CHARACTER EXCLUSIVE KEYBOARD SECRETS

these only work as the right character. type them as anyone else and nothing happens. each one is based on who the character really is.

UNEMPLOYED dante only
dante has no job, no office, no pension plan. type this and he celebrates it. "JACKPOT!!! INFINITE EVERYTHING HUURAH!!" blazes across the screen. gold slash storm. then +100 orbs and +500 score every single second for a full minute. he is not going to get a job. this is fine.
reward — +99999 score · +9999 orbs · +999 combo · gold chaos for 60 seconds
DEADWEIGHTBUM nero only
no spaces. type it all as one word. nero takes it personally and then proves every single meter wrong simultaneously. "INFINITE EVERYTHING METRE MAXED!!!" — rose meter, exceed gauge, everything. blue slash storm. orbs and score raining for a full minute. deadweight bum with maxed stats. say that again.
reward — +99999 score · +9999 orbs · rose meter 100 · exceed 100 · blue chaos for 60 seconds
CHAIR vergil only
just the word. vergil says "...the chair." and then cuts start spawning. and spawning. and spawning. one purple slash every single second for a full minute, concentration locked at 100 the entire time. infinite everything. he sat in the chair and ascended. that's the whole lore.
reward — +99999 score · +9999 orbs · concentration 100 forever · purple cuts every second for 60 seconds
— SECTION II —
RANK SECRETS
LDK RANK 100+ hit combo
reach 100 hits without dropping your combo and the rank letter transforms. the "D" through "SSS" system ends. what appears instead is not a rank — it's a title. the letter pulses between gold and white. "LEGENDARY DARK KNIGHT" announces itself with a gold slash storm. this is very hard to reach. that's intentional.
reward — gold pulsing rank letter · LEGENDARY DARK KNIGHT announce · gold slash storm
SSS TAUNT dante · 40+ combo · press taunt
taunting as dante while your combo is at 40 or above triggers a special visual. a crimson flash, five slashes, and a massive "SMOKIN' SEXY STYLE!!!" banner blazes across the screen with a full red glow. you didn't just taunt. you made a statement.
reward — crimson flash · full-screen banner · bragging rights
— SECTION III —
JUDGEMENT CUT END — VARIANTS

vergil's seven-cut chain has three possible endings depending on how you played in the moments before the final cut lands. the game watches. it remembers.

NORMAL default — just complete the chain
slashes build from sparse to chaotic. yamato ring tone plays and fades. silence hits. white screen. "JUDGEMENT CUT END" appears. the white gets cut by dark slashes until one single diagonal slash crosses the entire screen corner to corner — then the white dissolves into cuts and ceases to exist.
reward — +750 score base · +90 orbs · +concentration bonus
DARK VARIANT concentration 80+ when the 7th cut lands
vergil at absolute zero emotion. the screen goes black instead of white. cuts are bright white on darkness — completely inverted. the ring tone drops an octave. text slams in faster and harder. the single diagonal slash is pale white-purple against black. cuts dissolve the darkness into nothing. this is vergil when he stopped feeling anything.
reward — +750 score base · +90 orbs · dark variant announce · glass shatters white on black
MOTIVATED VARIANT taunted within 10 seconds before JCE fires
you said "show me your motivation." then you showed him. cuts fire in a perfect geometric radial pattern — like clock spokes, mathematically even, two full passes. no chaos, only precision. "...this is what it means." appears first before the title. the diagonal slash on the white is perfectly centred. everything is controlled. this is vergil when he is genuinely impressed.
reward — +750 score base · +90 orbs · special announce · longer silence · perfect geometry
— NOTE —

the motivated variant takes priority over the dark variant if both conditions are met. taunting at 80+ concentration and then completing the chain gives you motivated, not dark. vergil choosing to feel something overrides vergil feeling nothing. this is intentional.

— SECTION IV —
CHARACTER MECHANICS
BLUE ROSE CHARGE nero · press blue rose twice
first press charges. button pulses blue. you have three seconds to fire. second press releases a full charged beam — three overlapping screen tears, warp, shake. if you wait too long without firing: "charge lost — you snooze you lose." very nero.
reward — +13 combo · charged beam VFX · announce
EXCEED ENGINE nero · build exceed gauge with red queen
the red queen buttons change colour based on your exceed gauge. 30+ gauge — orange pulse. 60+ gauge — red flicker, engine overheating. 100 gauge — full white-orange fire, box shadow blazing. exceed slash at 30+ consumes the gauge for a major hit. at 100 gauge the showdown button begins screaming at you.
reward — visual feedback · exceed slash bonus damage · showdown unlock
DOPPELGANGER vergil · unlock in shop · press doppelganger
toggles the spectral clone. while active, all damage multiplied by 1.75. the doppelganger indicator appears on the right side of the screen and pulses. activating devil trigger while doppelganger is active dismisses it — vergil cannot sustain both states. he has to choose.
reward — ×1.75 damage multiplier on all moves while active
VERGIL TAUNT BONUS vergil · press taunt
unlike dante and nero whose taunts cost combo, vergil's taunt builds concentration by +25. the announce is "motivation — concentration surge!" taunting as vergil is a tactical decision, not showboating. also starts the 10-second window for the motivated JCE variant.
reward — +25 concentration · starts motivated JCE timer

— APPENDIX —
THINGS NOBODY TOLD YOU
THE PERFECT BADGE
the "PERFECT" badge in the top right corner of the screen flashes on a rhythm. hitting any move during that flash window gives a perfect timing bonus. +3 combo, +2 orbs, +60 score, and a gold announce. it pulses every 880ms. if you chain perfects, the bonus scales up.
VARIETY BONUS
the game tracks your last 5 moves. hitting the same button repeatedly gives less. mixing weapons and moves gives a variety multiplier up to +60%. the variety bar shows you how stylish you're being. playing like you actually know what you're doing is mechanically rewarded.
DEVIL BREAKER BREAK nero
every devil breaker visually breaks when used. the button goes struck-through and faded, crack lines fly off it. it recharges after 5 seconds. this is purely visual — but it tells you exactly what you just spent.
DANTE BULLET CINEMATIC
fire ebony and ivory six or more times in quick succession. if the bullet count hits the threshold, a cinematic fires automatically — no warning, no input. bullet holes appear on screen, a jackpot quote blazes in, everything shatters. it's not a move. it just happens when you push dante's guns far enough.
CONCENTRATION RESETS ON DT vergil
activating devil trigger as vergil resets concentration to zero. he is getting emotional. the game punishes this. play without DT and concentration compounds — every cut hits harder, every chain is worth more. the dark JCE variant requires 80+ concentration, which means playing restrained. the game is telling you something about the character.
